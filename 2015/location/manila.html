About

GDG Philippines will bring the I/O experience to different cities and help the professional and student developers deep dive in the new technologies. The Google I/O Extended Roadshow is a series of workshops and talks centered around the technologies announced during the Google I/O 2015. We'll have an amazing roster of speakers Googlers, Googler Developer Experts and more to come so stay tuned.

Speakers

Taylor Ling

Taylor is a professional Android Designer and passionate about good mobile UI/UX. Today, he works at Snappymob, a startup in Kuala Lumpur making a difference in the local mobile app development scene by creating high quality apps that users love. He started AndroidUIUX.com in early 2012 as a source of inspiration for Android developers and designers on Android Design topics, as well as offering free design resources.

Amit Chopra

Amit Chopra is the Regional Lead for the developer relations team for Google based out of Singapore. He is very passionate about making developer successful using Google technologies. He has coded and build many developer products over the last 20 years.

Wayne Manuel

Wayne Manuel is one of the community managers of Google Developers Group Philippines. He is the Chief Technology Officer ofthe National Telehealth Center at the University of the Philippines Manila. He also serves as a consultant for Rappler.com and forthe Department of Health.

Jomar Tigcal

Jomar Tigcal is an Android developer and one of the community managers for GDG Philippines. On his free time, he is developing personal applications using Android, other Google technologies, and other APIs, like Sweldong Pinoy.

Josan Astrid Dometita

Catherine Candano

Catherine Candano is responsible for strategic partnership development with developers & web/mobile publishers across Southeast Asia. At Google since 2011, Cathy specializes on data-driven partner growth, contributing as global lead on Google Analytics for Partners. Prior to Google, she's spent 8 years across a leading telecom in the Philippines, on teaching faculty in university, and digital outreach in 40+ Asian countries at the United Nations. She holds a double bachelors degrees in Economics-Honors & Communications from Ateneo de Manila University, and a masters degree in Communications & New Media from National University Singapore.

Ralph Vincent Regalado

Ralph Vincent Regalado is one of the community managers of Google Developers Group Philippines. He is an Assistant Professor of the College of Computer Studies of De La Salle University where he is also a faculty researcher of its Center for Language Technologies. He is also the CEO of Senti, a local startup that provides localized social media listening. He is a proud advocate of technopreneurship and a mountaineer at heart.

Kenneth Lingan

Elymar Apao

User Experience Manager of OLX.ph (formerly Sulit.com.ph), Founder of UX Philippines, Core member of the Philippine Web Designers Org

Mike Lu

Claudine Rodriguez

Claudine Rodriguez enjoys working on projects, and building mobile and web things that are conceptually playful but functional through Apparently, a product development studio she co-founded. On weekdays, she works as a UX and Innovations Designer for Havas Media Ortega, a media and advertising agency. During her free time, she loves making coffee (as a huge AeroPress fan), making music, and hanging out with their cats at home.
