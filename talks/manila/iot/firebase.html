Firebase

1 hour 30 minutes / Reymart Canuel

Firebase is expanding to become a unified app platform for Android, iOS and mobile web development. It includes features and integrations designed to help you develop your app, grow a user base, and earn money. At the heart of Firebase is Firebase Analytics, a free and unlimited analytics solution.
