Ralph Vincent RegaladoCommunity Manager, Google Developer Group Philippines

Ralph Vincent Regalado is one of the Community Managers of GDG Philippines. He is also the CEO and Founder of SENTi, a tech startup that analyzes sentiments and conversations in social media. He is a professor, and a Natural Language Processing researcher at De La Salle University, Manila. His research works were presented not only in local but also in international conferences hosted in China, Japan, Vietnam, Indonesia and the United States. When he is not at work, he spends his time travelling and hiking mountains.
