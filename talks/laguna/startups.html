Google Technologies for Startups

1 hour 30 minutes / Benj Tupas

The talk is about how to maximize Google technologies including Google Cloud Platform, Firebase, and Android. FunBuddies will be the case study of the talk and Benj will share about the lessons he learned while building the startup without a funding.

Speakers
Benj TupasFounder & CEO of FunBuddies

Benj is the Founder/CEO of FunBuddies. He loves technology, business, books, and travel/adventure.
