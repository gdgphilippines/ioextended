I/O Extended Series

The I/O Extended Series is hosted by GDG Philippines and interested Local Universities. It is a series of workshops and talks which share the latest technologies on web, mobile, design, cloud, big data and machine learning. Participants will also get to learn various technologies and updates from Google I/O 2016.

The events are hosted by local university partners who share the same passion in helping professional and student developers deep dive in the new technologies. The events will have roster of speakers ranging from Community Leaders, Developer Experts, to Googlers.

Last year's Series

Last year’s I/O Extended Events was hosted in 6 universities around the country: De La Salle University - Manila, De La Salle Lipa, Bicol University, Ateneo De Naga University, Holy Angel University, and De la Salle University - Dasmarinas. Participants were given the opportunity to hear talks from developer experts, community leaders and Googlers about emerging technologies in web and mobile development.

Aside from the talks, Code Labs were given in the afternoon. Participants were delighted to be able to learn different technologies including Android, HTML5, Polymer and many more.

Of course I/O Extended will never be complete without the freebies the participants received and the cool booths that were set up to enhance the I/O experience.

This year, GDG Philippines brings back the much-awaited roadshow to let more people have the I/O experience. See you there.

Live Viewing

Google I/O 2016 was held last May 18-20 at the Shoreline Amphitheatre in Mountain View, California. At its 10th year, Google I/O 2016 gathered more than 7,000 attendees from all over the world. Highlights of the event include the release of Google Home, Allo, Duo, Android N, and the new version of Firebase.
